# Refund policy (Forge — draft)

Forge processes refunds according to Stripe’s settlement timelines (**typically 5–10 business days** back to the original card).

- Automated paths (when implemented) honor clear duplicate charges and SLA windows described in-product.
- All other refunds are reviewed manually within one business week.
- Chargebacks/disputes are handled per Stripe Radar / issuing bank timelines.
