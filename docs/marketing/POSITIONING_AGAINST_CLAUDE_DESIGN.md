# Positioning — Forge War Room vs Claude Design (draft)

Internal narrative — revise before publishing.

## Claude Design emphasizes

Prompt-first UX, warm conversational chrome, artefact-centric tabs — speed to visual output.

## Forge War Room emphasizes

- **Orchestration traceability**: strategy, artefact synthesis, critiques visible as structure, not hidden chat prose.
- **System thinking**: monetization states, flows, overlays — surfaced as peers to the canvas.

## Audience

Operators who optimize **decisions and systems**, not just frames per minute.

Where overlap exists (responsive previews, marquee refine), Forge differentiates via **architecture** and **action discipline** (single Next move, bounded dock).
