# GlideDesign Identity

GlideDesign is the new product identity. It replaces the Forge identity across every user-facing surface.

## The Name

**GlideDesign** is one word. Use the capital `G` and `D` treatment in formal brand moments, navigation, metadata, legal pages, email headers, invoices, Open Graph images, and product chrome.

- Tagline: **Glide from idea to product.**
- Domain: `glidedesign.ai`
- Email: `hello@glidedesign.ai`
- Twitter/X: `@glidedesignai`

## Positioning

GlideDesign is the AI design tool that thinks like a senior product team and feels like Figma. You describe what you're building, and GlideDesign produces a complete plan, the screens, the code, and the next moves: instantly, beautifully, and ready to ship.

## Personality

GlideDesign is confident, playful, bright, generous, and futuristic. The voice should feel like Figma's marketing confidence met an AI product brain: visually fearless, but precise about outcomes.

## Voice And Tone

Use bold, direct language. Lead with big headlines, concrete verbs, and specific outcomes.

Do:

- Say what the product does in plain English.
- Use strong verbs: glide, build, ship, map, design, export, launch.
- Talk to designers, founders, and operators like they are smart.
- Name real deliverables: strategy, screens, code, exports, next moves.

Do not:

- Hedge with phrases like "might consider exploring."
- Use generic hype such as "revolutionary" or "game-changing."
- Sound like a chat sandbox, a no-code builder, or a toy for amateurs.

## What We Are Not

- **Not Figma:** GlideDesign is not a manual design tool. It produces outcomes from intent.
- **Not Claude Design:** GlideDesign is not a chat sandbox. It plans, designs, builds, and routes the next move.
- **Not Webflow:** GlideDesign is not a no-code site builder. It generates product strategy, interfaces, code, and exports.
- **Not AI design for amateurs:** GlideDesign is for serious operators who want more leverage without lowering craft standards.

| **Serious** | Claude Design | Webflow |

GlideDesign sits in the playful + outcome-shaped quadrant: we ship products, with joy.

## Permission To Echo Figma

GlideDesign deliberately echoes Figma's marketing aesthetic: chunky color, bold type, generous whitespace, real product imagery used as design, and playful primitive shapes.

Figma solved a hard problem: making a design tool feel exciting at first glance. GlideDesign adopts those principles, not the literal pixels. We do not reproduce Figma's logo, specific layouts, screenshots, or copy verbatim. We translate the energy into a distinct AI product experience.

## Product Promise

GlideDesign should feel like a 10x experience compared with ordinary AI design tools:

- It understands the product before drawing the screen.
- It treats design, code, copy, and workflow as one system.
- It gives the next useful action instead of leaving the user staring at a blank canvas.
- It feels fast, colorful, alive, and serious about craft.
