# Forge — Identity Foundation (canonical)

**Source of truth.** When visual or voice choices conflict elsewhere, **this document wins**.

---

## Positioning

Forge is the fastest way to take a product from idea to live mini-app. Where competitors feel like a conversation about design, Forge feels like a **workshop** where the product gets built — focused tooling, deliberate motion, outputs you can publish.

---

## Personality

**We are:** focused, tooled, professional, warm (human, not sentimental), deliberate.

**We are not:** cold, generic, hyper-corporate, playful-to-a-fault, or “AI demo” slick.

---

## Voice & tone

- **Direct verbs**, plain nouns — “Publish page”, “Download zip”.
- **Specific over vague** — name the constraint, quantity, reset time.
- **Plain over corporate** — say what broke and what to do next.
- **Honest over promotional** — no hollow praise inside the product.
- **Product chrome**: no emoji, no unnecessary exclamation marks. (User-authored content may differ.)

---

## Visual register — “workshop materials”

- **Surfaces**: warm bone and sand layers — neutral-warm whites, lower chroma than “cream competitor” palettes.
- **Ink**: workshop graphite — deep warm gray, not pure black.
- **Primary accent**: copper — metal and precision, deliberately **not** terracotta/clay competitor cues.
- **Data / system pane**: emerald-warm — analytics, diagrams, SYSTEM indicators (distinct reading path from commerce accent).

Forge is visually **warm but tooled**. Calm restraint; nothing reads as conversational “pillow UI.”

---

## Motion register

Deliberate, professional — motion signals **system state**. No decorative looping motion on resting chrome. Reduced motion disables non-essential animation; interactions remain instantaneous.

See [MOTION_SYSTEM.md](./MOTION_SYSTEM.md).

## What we are *not* (adjacent-brand shorthand)

This is orientation, not teardowns.

| Not | Emotional read |
|-----|----------------|
| Claude Design | Conversational warmth + cream terracotta “thinking” cues |
| Linear | Stark cool austerity + icy indigo |
| Notion | Casual blocks-on-cream |
| v0-style | Flat white / black technical minimal |
| Figma-forward | Neutral gray tooling + multicolor sprawl |

## 2×2 — conversational ↔ tooled × warm ↔ cool

```
            warm
              │
              │
 conversational● Claude / chat-first tools
              │
              │
tooled ───────┼─────── exploratory
              │
              ● Forge               ● Linear-ish (cool/tool)
    cool      │
```

- **Forge** — **warm + tooled** (workshop quadrant).
- **Conversation-first tools** skew warm + conversational.
- **Highly tooled minimalist products** skew cool.

---

## Change control

Altering primary surfaces, typography roles, accent hue, motion timings, or product voice rules is a **design-system change**. Update this foundation first; then cascade tokens and docs.
