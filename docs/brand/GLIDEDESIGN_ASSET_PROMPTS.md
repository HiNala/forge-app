# GlideDesign Asset Direction And Prompt Bank

This file is the source prompt ledger for GlideDesign marketing and in-app art. Use it when generating bitmap assets, extending the SVG asset set, or reviewing whether an image belongs in the product. The reference is Figma's confidence with GlideDesign's product-brain subject matter: chunky color, generous whitespace, oversized UI, playful geometry, and actual product surfaces rather than generic AI swirls.

## Global Art Direction

- Product first: every asset should show a real surface, product output, or tool state.
- Color is fearless: lime, sky, coral, mint, lavender, mustard, magenta, and deep ink.
- Shapes are deliberate: circles, squares, brackets, checkerboards, dotted connectors, cursor arrows, and UI selection handles.
- Typography is bold and geometric. Use General Sans / Inter style letterforms.
- Avoid stock SaaS tropes: no floating dashboards in blue gradients, no robot heads, no abstract cloud brains.
- Make it feel usable: frames, panels, buttons, cards, cursor labels, collaborator chips, code snippets, and export affordances should look designed.

## Core Marketing Assets

### Homepage Hero Product Composite

Prompt:
> High-fidelity product marketing illustration for GlideDesign, an AI design tool. Show a large app window with three clear panes: Strategy on the left, a central canvas with multiple product screens, and System/data model on the right. Use a white x.com-clear app interior, Figma-style chunky color accents, violet-to-coral brand gradient, mint data accents, collaborator cursor labels, polished shadows, crisp UI details, and a bold caption area. No robots, no generic AI glow, no stock people.

Acceptance:
- The first two seconds communicate "AI product design tool."
- The UI panes are legible at marketing size.
- The art is colorful but not childish.

### Chunky Template Card: Websites

Prompt:
> Figma-inspired chunky website template card for GlideDesign. Bright lime full-bleed background, large browser-like landing page screenshot, real hero section, CTA pills, product blocks, overlay panel, playful geometric shapes, black ink typography, white cards, violet/coral accents, crisp cursor detail. Looks like a shipped template, not a placeholder.

### Chunky Template Card: Mobile Apps

Prompt:
> Saturated sky-blue GlideDesign template card showing three mobile app screens with flow connectors. Screens show onboarding, discovery, and action states. Use black phone frames, white interiors, violet/coral brand accents, lime and lavender chips, collaborator cursor marker, bold "mobile flows" label.

### Chunky Template Card: Pitch Decks

Prompt:
> Vivid coral GlideDesign pitch deck card with overlapping investor deck slides. Include a Series A title slide, a traction chart, colorful circular product metrics, and a small arrow chart. Bold geometric Figma-like art direction, crisp shadows, serious but playful.

### Chunky Template Card: Forms

Prompt:
> Lavender GlideDesign form builder template card. Show a form with polished input rows, logic branch dotted connectors, a dark analytics/automation side panel, star/spark motif, and violet submit button. Make the form feel useful and shippable.

### Chunky Template Card: Proposals

Prompt:
> Mustard GlideDesign proposal card. Show a business proposal document, clear scope sections, pricing highlight, signature panel, and dark proof card. Use white paper, black ink, violet/coral/mint accents, and Figma-style chunky background geometry.

## In-App Empty State Set

Generate these in one family: soft-fill vector/raster hybrid, white surface, brand-gradient cursor motif, one strong object per state, no dense detail.

- Dashboard empty: a cursor drawing the first product frame.
- Submissions empty: an envelope with a gradient dot trail entering it.
- Analytics empty: a chart waiting to fill, with one mint line beginning.
- Templates filter empty: a search lens over colored template tiles.
- Memory empty: a small archive of preference cards with editable sliders.
- API tokens empty: a key made from a cursor arrow and gradient dot.
- Webhooks empty: connected nodes with one unplugged endpoint.
- Custom domains empty: a globe with a small branded route line.
- Team members empty: three avatar circles around a shared cursor.
- Billing invoices empty: a receipt stack with a violet paid stamp.

## Error State Set

- 404: cursor looking at a broken path sign, white background, violet/coral accents.
- 500: product window exhaling a soft gradient pulse, direct recovery CTA space.
- 503: paused build controls and resting shapes, calm maintenance mood.
- 401: friendly lock with bright keyhole, direct sign-in action.
- 403: workspace doorway with a soft barrier and alternate workspace chip.

## Review Rubric

- Would this sit comfortably beside the Figma homepage references?
- Does it show product capability instead of decoration?
- Is the color memorable without hurting readability?
- Would a stranger understand GlideDesign makes products from prompts?
- Does it feel clearly GlideDesign, not Figma, Claude, Webflow, or generic SaaS?
